- About Me -

I hope to help create indulgent and performant websites and web applications for all mediums, be it commercial, personal, creative and even fashion.

My current design approach ranges from minimalism and simplicity to colorful, fancy and illustrative types. Of course, I'm determined to acquire more varieties of design.

Being very precise in whatever I do is a trait I am quite proud of, but is also a weakness because sometimes I dwell on details a little bit much.

In my free time, I enjoy baking, drinking tea or coffee, playing mobile games (I love Sky by thatgamecompany), watching animes and Korean shows.

- A little history -

- Born in Palermo.
- Raised in a small town in the Philippines.
- Been living in Milan for 10 years.

Growing up as a shy child, I found out that the best way to express myself was through drawing and creating. I used to sketch lots of personal imaginary characters and invented house layouts. I also tried to explore fashion once and made my own clothes.

Design has always fascinated me. That's why I began engaging in this world and ultimately decided to work on the digital/web side. I started my learning journey in University of Milan and then turned to self-teaching.
